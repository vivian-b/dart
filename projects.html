Final

AAH is a fictional news website focused on articles surrounding the topic of Anti-Asian hate. The website imitates a news outlet and focuses solely on events relating to Asian-hate. Hidden interactions are added to reflect the long-standing history of the issue that needs to be discovered to better understand the roots that led to the present day sentiments. Users are invited to travel around the page and discover the interactions and revisit the element that triggered such reactions. The blurring of the terms surrounding racism reflect how this issue is often swept under the rug and downplayed. The fading of the words Asia/Asian reflects how the community wishes to hide their identity because of the discrimination they face.
