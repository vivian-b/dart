Interactions

"Discreet facts or stories will have to be 'dug up' or discovered by the user. The old stories, dating back to the 19th or 20th century, will be hidden underneath the news or camouflaged somewhere on the page. The interaction encourages the user to find out more about the topic and how all current events are linked to past ones. All information will be linked to the designated article it was sourced from for the user to, hopefully, find out more about the event on their own." – proposal.pdf
